📖 Section 1 — Parichay

Consumer kaun hota hai?

Har woh insaan jo koi cheez khareedta hai ya service leta hai — woh Consumer hai. Market se sabzi, ya app se order — sab consumer hain.

🛒 Consumer = Khareedaar

Jo goods ya services personal use ke liye purchase karta hai — woh consumer hai. Business ke liye nahi.

🛡️ Rights Kyun Zaroori?

Takki koi bhi company ya dukandaar aapko cheat na kar sake. Fairness aur safety ensure ho.

⚠️ Bina Rights ke?

Nakli product, galat price, kharab service — sab chalta rahega agar aap chup rahein.

💪 Rights ke Saath

Aap complain kar sakte ho, compensation maang sakte ho aur Consumer Forum mein justice pa sakte ho.

✅ Section 3 — Jimmedari

Consumer ki Zimmedariyan

Rights ke saath responsibilities bhi aati hain. Ek smart consumer hamesha ye karta hai:

🧾

Bill Zaroor Lo

Har kharidari pe receipt ya invoice lo — yahi aapka proof hai. Bina bill ke koi case nahi banta.

🔍

Product Check Karo

Kharidne se pehle product ko ache se dekhna. Packaging seal, ISI mark, ya certification check karo.

📅

Expiry Date Dekho

Food, medicine ya cosmetics kharidne se pehle expiry date zaroor check karo. Expired cheez mat lo.

💡

Aware Raho

Apne rights jaano, fake deals se bachke raho, aur agar kuch galat ho — chup mat raho. Awaaz uthao.

📜 Section 4 — Kanoon

Consumer Laws in India

Sarkar ne aapki protection ke liye strong laws banaye hain. Ye jaanna zaroori hai:

📜 Parliament Act of India

Consumer Protection Act, 2019

  • ✓
    Teen levels pe Consumer Courts — District (20L tak), State (1Cr tak), National (1Cr se zyada)
  • ✓
    E-commerce aur online purchases cover — Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho sab included
  • ✓
    Product Liability — manufacturer, seller aur service provider directly zimmedaar hain
  • ✓
    Unfair Trade Practices ke against strict punishment aur fine
  • ✓
    Mediation ka option — bina court ke settlement bhi possible hai
  • ✓
    Complaint 2 saal ke andar file karni hoti hai, zyada delay mat karo

⚠️ Online Fraud Protection

2019 Act mein pehli baar e-commerce ko cover kiya gaya. Agar online fake product mila, delivery nahi aayi, ya payment fraud hua — toh Consumer Forum mein case file kar sakte hain. Fake reviews promote karna bhi unfair practice aur punishable hai.

📣 Section 5 — Complaint Process

Complaint Kaise Karein?

Agar aapke saath kuch galat hua — ghabrao mat. Step by step ye process follow karo:

1

Evidence Collect Karo

Bill, receipt, photos, screenshots, chat history — sab save karo. Yeh aapka sabse important weapon hai.

2

Company Ko Directly Complain Karo

Customer care ko email ya written complaint do. Proof ke liye hamesha written mein karo, verbal nahi.

3

National Helpline Pe Call Karo

Free helpline pe call karo. Trained advisors aapki madad karenge aur aage ka process batayenge.

4

Online Portal Pe Register Karo

consumerhelpline.gov.in ya edaakhil.nic.in pe online complaint file kar sakte ho — bilkul free.

5

Consumer Forum Mein Jaao

Agar kuch resolve nahi hua, District Consumer Forum mein case file karo. 20 lakh tak yahan resolve hota hai.

💡 Section 6 — Real Examples

Real Zindagi ke Cases

Yeh situations aapke saath bhi ho sakti hain — jaano kya karna chahiye:

⚠️ Nakli Product

Duplicate Brand ka Shampoo

Rahul ne online ek branded shampoo order kiya ₹450 mein. Mila bilkul alag product with fake packaging. Company ne refund deny kar diya.

✅ Kya kare: Bill + photo save karo. edaakhil.nic.in pe complaint file karo. Refund + compensation milega.
💻 Online Scam

Fake Delivery Call Fraud

Priya ko call aaya "Aapka parcel customs mein ruka hai, ₹800 transfer karo." Usne paise bheje, call cut gaya. Total fraud tha.

✅ Kya kare: Kabhi call pe paise mat bhejo. Cyber Crime helpline 1930 pe call karo. cybercrime.gov.in pe report karo.
💰 Overpricing

MRP Se Zyada Price Charge

Sonu ne railway station pe water bottle kharidi ₹50 mein, jabki MRP ₹20 thi. Dukandaar bola "yahan aisa hi hota hai."

✅ Kya kare: MRP se zyada price lena illegal hai. Photo lo, receipt maango, 1800-11-4000 pe call karo.
